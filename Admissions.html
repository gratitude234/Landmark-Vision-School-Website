Admissions Process

At Landmark Vision School, we are excited to welcome new students who are eager to explore and grow in a nurturing and stimulating environment. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

Step 1: Inquiry & Application

Start by reaching out to our admissions office or submitting an inquiry form online. Once you’ve received all the necessary information, fill out the application form and submit it along with the required documents.

Step 2: Assessment & Interview

After reviewing your application, we will schedule an assessment and interview to understand your child’s strengths and how they can benefit from our programs.

Step 3: Admission Decision

Following the assessment and interview, our admissions team will review the application and inform you of the decision. Upon acceptance, you will receive further instructions to complete the enrollment process.
