About Us

Welcome to Landmark Vision School, where we believe that every child has the potential to achieve greatness. Located at Iyaniwura Street, Abusoro, Akure, our school has been a beacon of educational excellence since its founding on 7th September 2008.

At Landmark Vision School, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds through a balanced approach to education that emphasizes both academic rigor and character development. Our mission is to inspire lifelong learning and foster an environment where curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking are encouraged.

Join us at Landmark Vision School, where the vision for a brighter future begins today.
